GRAMMAR:EXPRESSING PRESENT AND PAST TENSE

Present tense are tense that expresses actions that happened always, regularly, frequently etc. Past tense are tense expresses actions that happened in the past.

present past
1. I will come to your house tomorrow 1. I came to your house yesterday.
2. I wash my clothes every Saturday. 2. I washed my Cloth on Saturday.
3. I will buy biscuit for you. 3. I bought biscuit for you.
4. I Pray to God every morning. 4. I prayed in the morning.
5. I travel to Dubai once in every month. 5. I traveled to Dubai last month
