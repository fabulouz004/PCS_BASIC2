GRAMMAR:SIMPLE PRESENT AND PAST TENSE.

Tense is the time in which an action took place. We have two main tense in the English studies, they are present and past tense. The present tense is used to talk about what you do everyday and things and things to be done in the future, while past tense is used for actions that have been carried out.

A GOAT

A goats is a domestic animal it has legs, two ears and two horns. A goat also has two eyes, a nose, a mouth and a text. Goats eat grasses, backs of yam and corn. The female goat is called nanny, while the male goat is called Billy. The baby goat is called kid. A goat bleats, goats live in pens, goats meat can be used in cooking stew, soups or any other delicacies.
