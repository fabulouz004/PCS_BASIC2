COUNTING OF WHOLE NUMBERS 1_200

All the numbers from 1 to 200 are whole numbers.
eg 3, 4, 10, 23, 56, 196, 175, ……

Prime numbers are numbers that can only be divided by itself and 1.ie, 2 cannot divide it, 3 cannot divide it etc.
eg 2, 3, 5, 7, 11, 13, 17, 19, 23, 27, 31, 37, 41, 43, 44, 53, 57, 59, 61, 67, 71, 73, 79, 81, 83, 87, 89, 91, 92.

Perfect numbers are numbers that are multiplied by itself.
eg, 1, 4, 9, 12, 25, 36, 49, 64, 81, 100, 121, 144, 126, 196, 335, 256, 289, 324, 361, 400, 441, 484, 529, 576, 625.
