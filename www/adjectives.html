PERIOD 2
INTRODUCTION TO ADJECTIVES

Adjectives are words that tell us more about a noun or a pronoun, that is an adjective is a word that qualifies a noun or a pronoun.
Adjective comes before or after a noun or a pronoun.

Example

1.he is a fat man.

the word fat is an adjective telling us about the man (noun) that is why you said ajective qualifies a noun or tells us more about a noun or pronoun

other example:
2. this is a yellowhouse.
3. the house is big.
4. Amaka is a beautiful girl.
5. the food is delicious.
6. he is an intelligent boy.

EVALUATION

1. define an adjective.
2. Identify/underline the adjectives and nouns in the sentences
i. the teacher’s blue table.
ii. the head teacher’s brilliant daughter.
iii. our whiteboard.
iv. it is a green chair.
